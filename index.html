Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science Latin America

Our Community

The Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science Latin America is a community and non-profit meeting, aiming at promoting and making visible the research carried out by women in the fields of Bioinformatics and Data Science. It also seek to create networking and collaboration within the community of women scientists. This congress will bring together researchers from Latin America who work in the areas of systems biology, omics technologies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, data mining and high-performance computing with applications in biology and the public and private sectors.
