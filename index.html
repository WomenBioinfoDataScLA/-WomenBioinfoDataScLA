Our Community

We are a community of women working in the fields of Bioinformatics and Data Science created in 2019 aiming at promoting and making visible the research carried out by women in these fields. We also seek to create networking and collaboration within the community of women scientists from both the public and private sectors.

Our Goals

  • To promote and visibilize women research in the Bioinformatics and Data Science fields
  • To encourage women to establish collaborative research networks
  • To empower young scientists to be confident in their work and capabilities
